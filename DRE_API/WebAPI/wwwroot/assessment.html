Solar Radiation & Power Production Assessment (A1, B1)


Our Service Provides Two Levels of Solar Data Insights
  • Basic Assessment – Visualize and download monthly average values of Global Horizontal Irradiance (GHI) and Direct Normal Irradiance (DNI) for any user-defined location and date range. This tool helps users analyze solar resource availability with no cost.
  • Premium Assessment – In addition to solar radiation data, this service offers power production estimation based on your solar park’s specifications, including panel tilt, azimuth, tracking type (fixed, single-axis, or dual-axis), and capacity.

Data Source: CAMS Solar Radiation Data Service.

Retrieve Monthly Mean Solar Radiation Data (A1)

Retrieve monthly mean values of Global Horizontal Irradiance (GHI) and Direct Normal Irradiance (DNI) for your location. Simply enter your latitude and longitude, along with the start and end dates, to generate a detailed visualization of solar radiation trends. You can also download the data in CSV format for further analysis. This tool helps optimize solar energy planning by providing essential insights into site-specific solar resource availability.

Retrieve Monthly Mean Solar Radiation & Energy Output Data (B1)

Get solar radiation data and energy production estimates tailored to your solar park. Enter your latitude and longitude, along with the start and end dates, to generate a detailed visualization of Global Horizontal Irradiance (GHI) and Direct Normal Irradiance (DNI). Additionally, provide your solar park specifications—including panel tilt, azimuth, tracking type (fixed, single-axis, or dual-axis), and capacity—to receive the corresponding power production estimation. You can also download the data in CSV format for further analysis.
